# **App Name**: Demyanenko Team Hub

## Core Features:

- User Registration and Roles: Secure registration and authentication system with roles for athletes, coaches, parents, and administrators, each with specific permissions.
- Interactive Training Schedule: Dynamic training schedule that athletes, coaches, and parents can view. Includes booking, attendance tracking, and notifications.
- Personalized Training Recommendations: AI-driven tool that uses athlete performance data and attendance to generate tailored training recommendations. Here, the AI uses a tool to decide when to incorporate information into the recommendations.
- Integrated Payment System: Secure payment gateway for membership fees, training sessions, and access to exclusive content, with options for auto-renewal.
- Certification and Competition Management: System to manage certification and competition registrations, schedules, and results.
- Hall of Fame Showcase: Interactive "Hall of Fame" section highlighting the team's top athletes, their achievements, and biographies.

## Style Guidelines:

- Основной цвет: Белый (#FFFFFF), для чистоты и минимализма.
- Вторичный цвет: Синий (#2962FF), для акцентов и интерактивных элементов.
- Акцентный цвет: Красный (#FF1744), для важных уведомлений и действий.
- Шрифт заголовка: 'Kanit', современный и смелый шрифт.
- Шрифт текста: 'Mitr', удобный и легко читаемый шрифт.
- Пользовательские иконки в стиле flat design, с использованием основных цветов.
- Адаптивный дизайн с акцентом на мобильные устройства и удобство навигации.
